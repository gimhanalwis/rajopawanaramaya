About Us

Sri Amarawansa Dhamma School


To further uplift the standard of Buddhist education, the Getambe Rajopavanaramaya also manages Sri Amarawansa Dhamma School in premises. It was was inaugurated by Ven.Yatalamaththe Sri Amarawansha Nayaka Thera. Amarawansa Dhamma School accommodates around 3,000 students under the care of 69 committed teachers Which is divided into senior and junior sections and runs parallel to a separate English Dhamma School as well. This school is a free education Buddhist religion center (Sunday School) for children which intends to produce non-violent, peaceful, well-mannered students with their ambitions to become useful people to the world.


Staff

Sri Amarawansa Dhamma School


The staff consists of 76 teachers where many of them are holding Bachelor's degrees . The chief monk Venerable kappetiyagoda siriwimala thera is the principal of this school where they conduct both english and sinhala medium classes. The teachers in Sri Amarawansha Dhamma School don't take any payment for their service as they consider it to be a service to the society and a charity work. The school has proven its education standards in many ways .The students have won several islandwide and regional certifications and awards.
