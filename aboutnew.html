History

Sri rajopawanaramaya is one of the most oldest temples in kandy, sri lanka.
Started by ven.Yatamaththe Chandrakeerthi sri dharma amarawansha nayaka thera in late 19th century.
Its situated in gatambe, peradeniya nearby the scenic mahaweli river. This Buddhist temple includes several imposing buildings and is situated in gatambe nearby the placid waters of mahaweli river.
It has the main features of a Vihara (temple), the Cetiya (Pagada) the Bodhitree, the Vihara Mandiraya, the Seema malaka (assembly hall for monks) and the Relic Chamber. In addition, a library, a residential hall, Pirivena, educational halls and an alms hall are also on the premises
